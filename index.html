About me

Hi there! My name is Oleksiy Letushev and I am a 21-year-old web developer. Currently, I am living and working in Kyiv, Ukraine.

In my childhood, I was into painting. I was so obsessed with Vincent van Gogh and his art, that I recreated a lot of his masterpieces by myself.
In school, I had no problems with maths, informatics, and physics, so I decided to study some technical specialization at university.
One year ago I graduated from the Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv with a bachelor's degree in computer science. That was an exciting and sometimes extremely challenging part of my life. I am very thankful to the university for giving me such important fundamental programming skills: C++, C#, ASP.NET, Java, Python. What's more, at the Faculty of Computer Science and Cybernetics, I was studying a lot of further mathematics stuff. That was okay, I love maths, but that was not exactly what I wanted. I missed creating something visually beautiful.

I remember the first book I read about web development - HTML and CSS: Design and Build Websites by Jon Duckett. This book just made me fall in love with creating beautiful websites and that was the beginning of my journey as a front-end developer.

I have finished a great practical course Kottans, which gave me a strong understanding of HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript.

Then I have been working as a front-end developer at ЛУН for one year. There I've gained a lot of experience working in a team with a big complex project. I have mastered plenty of new skills and tools: Git, GitHub, Webpack, React, MobX, SCSS, and many more.

I have learned how to:

  • build responsive user-friendly layouts
  • create beautiful animations
  • build UI with reusable React components
  • communicate with a server
  • manage complex data state in a browser
  • work with multilingualism and so on

My passion for web development is rapidly growing, so I try to learn and practice new skills and techniques every day.

In my spare time, I love playing the guitar , watching movies , listening to all kinds of good music , running , or doing any other sport activity. Also, I am extremely passionate about hiking . I dream of becoming a professional web developer and teaching other people how to do this kind of magic .
